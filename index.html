Résumé

Originally trained as designer at Grafisch Lyceum in Amsterdam, experienced in print, film, video games and software development. Paul has a special interest in new media and digital interfaces.

Consultant Digital Concepts

Client history includes Beslist, Albert Heijn and Seat via Buyerminds. adidas Global and Heineken Global via Tribal DDB. Vodafone and Philips via Chunk.

Co-founder W!Games

Paul succesfully created the initial presentations for investors, employees and other stakeholders.

W!Games was a computer game developer based in Amsterdam. It released games on Nintendo Wii, Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. In 2010, W!Games merged into Vanguard Entertainment, in turn merging with Force Field VR, and most recently with Vertigo Games.

Co-founder, CTO Woedend!

Paul successfully built interactive media production and software development teams, introduced production pipelines for digital marketing and advertising for first internet marketing and e-commerce platforms.

Woedend! became the first independent global advertising agency in the Netherlands in 2007.

Clients include ASICS global, Onitsuka Tiger (silver cyber lion at Cannes), adidas Benelux, ABN AMRO, AJAX, Philips, Expert, Mexx, VNU Sp!ts, Pfizer, Soa Aids Nederland, Venz/Heinz, Red Bull, Sourcy Vitaminwater, Pepsico, Heineken, Amstel, ING, Zwitserleven, Manpower, BMW motors, Yamaha Motors Europe and Kia Motors Nederland.

Founder Outlander Studio

At Outlander Paul directed and produced music videos and multimedia productions with a focus on 3D motion graphics.

Clients include Glitch (P&G, 2 Unlimited/Byte Records, TMF Megadance, eSpaces), Pierôt Coiffures and CHemistry Amsterdam.

Demo Jockey @ Trigital Amsterdam

At Trigital, Paul helped introduce digital creation tools in the Netherlands in the late 1990s.

Clients inlcude Danielle Kwaaitaal, Micha Klein, NOS and the Royal Netherlands Navy.

Tools include DPS Perception/Reality, Sony digital Betacam, Intergraph TDZ 2000, Silicon Graphics 320/540, Eyeon Digital Fusion, Autodesk/Kintex 3D Studio Max and Alias|Wavefront Maya.
